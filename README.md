This project analyzes ten years of diabetic patient hospitalization data from 130 U.S. hospitals to identify patterns and drivers of readmission within 30 days. The analysis focuses on patient demographics, clinical complexity, hospital department performance, and specialty-level risk, combining data cleaning, feature engineering, pivot tables, and interactive Excel dashboards. Insights support targeted interventions, optimized care coordination, and evidence-based hospital decision-making to reduce readmission rates and improve patient outcomes.
